Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Snoreline - Snore Diary is an offline-first app. All data you enter (logs, notes, and settings) is stored locally on your device only, in your device's application data storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no ad networks, and no third-party servers involved in this app's core functionality.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed with it.
